About Me

After graduating College I decided to follow my passion for professional table tennis. My desire for helping others and teaching took me in the direction of coaching. I take pride in the relationships I develop with my students and clients. One of my greatest accomplishments throughout my career has been the positive impact I have had on the lives of many of my young students.
I gained my first basic coding experience while seeking new ways to build our marketing division's user outreach at Indian Community Center. I quickly fell in love with the process of web development. I decided that I enjoyed coding enough that I would like to pursue it full-time. After self-studying and graduating a full-time Coding Bootcamp I am proficient in a wide range of coding languages and technologies.
I take pride in each step I take and my individual impact on a company. I believe technology has the power to shape society and positivity impact global culture. My goal is to be a part of the change that helps to make this world a more productive, and peaceful place.
I am proficient in four full development stacks: Python with Django and Flask Framework, MEAN stack, MERN stack, and Java with Spring Framework.
